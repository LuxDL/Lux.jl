Selected 3rd Party Tutorials ​

WARNING

These tutorials are developed by the community and may not be up-to-date with the latest version of Lux.jl. Please refer to the official documentation for the most up-to-date information.

Please open an issue (ideally both at Lux.jl and at the downstream linked package) if any of them are non-functional and we will try to get them updated.

GPU-Accelerated Physics-Informed Neural Networks

Use Machine Learning (PINNs) to solve the Heat Equation PDE on a GPU.

Weather Forecasting with Neural ODEs

Train a neural ODEs to a multidimensional weather dataset and use it for weather forecasting.

Controlling Stochastic Differential Equations

Control the time evolution of a continuously monitored qubit described by an SDE with multiplicative scalar noise.

Medical Image Segmentation

Explore various aspects of deep learning for medical imaging and a comprehensive overview of Julia packages.

Neural PDE closures

Learn an unknown term in a PDE using convolutional neural networks and Fourier neural operators.
